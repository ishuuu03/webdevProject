Reaching the Nations

At Light and Life Medical Center, our mission extends far beyond physical healing. Inspired by the compassion of Jesus Christ, we are committed to reaching out to those in need—serving with kindness, dignity, and unwavering care. Just as Jesus cared for the poor and reached the untouched, we strive to reflect that same love through every service we offer.


We believe that no one should be denied access to quality healthcare due to financial hardship or lack of insurance. That’s why we proudly provide a range of free medical services to underserved and struggling families in our community. These services are listed below, as part of our promise to bring hope, healing, and health to all.
